Rebalance Portfolio

Split free cash across your stocks to move toward your target allocation.

  1. Enter each stock's current value (₪) and your desired allocation (%).
  2. Targets must sum to 100%.
  3. Enter the free cash you want to invest, then click Calculate.
  4. The Buy column shows how much to purchase per stock, rounded to ₪10.
